		FYI, for folks on UNIXes, you might want to change your
debug instructions from "python SPE.py --debug > debug.txt" to "python
SPE.py --debug > debug.txt 2>&1" 
		
		Without that '2>&1', items written to STDOUT go to the
file debug.txt, and items written to STDERR (the stack trace) goes to
the console, which is why you might be getting disjoint info.
